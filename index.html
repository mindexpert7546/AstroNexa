Is online consultation available?

Yes, we offer both online and offline consultations. You can connect with our expert astrologers via phone call, WhatsApp video call, or visit us in person. Online consultations are just as detailed and effective as in-person sessions.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling +91 83022 12616, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form on this page. We confirm your appointment within a few hours and schedule you at the earliest available slot.

What details are required for consultation?

For a detailed consultation we need your full name, date of birth, exact time of birth, and place of birth. This helps us create an accurate Kundli (birth chart) to provide personalized guidance and predictions.

How long does a consultation take?

A standard consultation typically lasts 30–60 minutes depending on the complexity of your situation. We ensure every client receives sufficient time to discuss their problems and receive thorough, actionable guidance.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We treat all client information with utmost confidentiality. Your personal details, birth information, and consultation discussions are never shared with any third party. Your privacy is our commitment.
